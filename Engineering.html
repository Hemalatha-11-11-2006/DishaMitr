National Defence Academy (NDA)

Eligibility: 12th pass with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics

Age: 16.5 to 19.5 years

Selection Process: NDA Written Exam followed by SSB Interview

After Selection

  • 3 years training at NDA, Khadakwasla
  • Commissioned as Officer in Indian Army, Navy or Air Force
  • B.Tech degree awarded after completion

Advantages

  • Prestigious career in defence services
  • No tuition fees - stipend during training
  • Assured employment after training
  • Respect and job security
