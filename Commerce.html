B.Com (Bachelor of Commerce)

3-year undergraduate degree focusing on accountancy, economics, business law. Suitable for careers in finance, banking, and further studies.

  • Core SubjectsAccounting, Economics, Law, Business Management
  • Duration3 Years
  • Entrance ExamCUET (for admissions in many JK colleges)
×

BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration)

3-year undergraduate degree centered on business management, marketing, and HR. Opens opportunities in management traineeships and startups.

  • SpecializationsMarketing, HR, Finance, International Business
  • Duration3 Years
  • Entrance ExamCUET or Merit-Based Admission
×

CA (Chartered Accountant)

Professional course offering expertise in auditing, taxation, finance, and accounting. Rigorous exams and practical training over 3+ years.

  • Key StagesCPT, IPCC, Final Exams
  • Duration3-5 Years (depends on clearing exams)
  • Entrance ExamCPT or Foundation Level for Beginners
×

CS (Company Secretary)

Professional qualification in company law, secretarial practice, and governance. Important for legal compliance and corporate leadership roles.

  • Focus AreasCompany Law, Corporate Governance, Tax Laws
  • Duration3-4 Years
  • Entrance ExamCS Foundation
×

Banking & Finance

Jobs in banks (like J&K Bank), finance companies & insurance sectors. Roles vary from clerks to financial advisors. Strong demand in J&K region.

  • SpecializationsRetail Banking, Risk Management, Insurance
  • DurationVaries - UG & PG courses (2-3 Years)
  • Entrance ExamsIBPS, SBI PO, JK Bank Recruitment Exams
×

Accounts & Auditing

Accountant, auditor, and tax consultant roles across businesses & government. Professional courses such as CA & CS enhance prospects.

  • SpecializationsTaxation, Auditing, Cost Accounting
  • Duration3-5 Years (Professional courses vary)
  • Entrance ExamsNone for B.Com; Professional exams for CA/CS
×

Business Development

Growth roles in startups, SMEs & retail. Involves sales, client management, partnerships and market expansion in J&K.

  • Focus AreasClient Management, Sales Strategy, Market Analysis
  • Duration3 Years (UG Degree)
  • Entrance ExamsGenerally Merit-based or CUET
×

Sales & Marketing

Marketing, branding, and sales roles across FMCG, retail, telecom, and manufacturing in J&K. Digital marketing is a growing field.

  • SpecializationsDigital Marketing, Sales Management, Advertising
  • Duration3 Years (UG Degree)
  • Entrance ExamsMostly Merit or CUET-based
×

E-Commerce & Digital

E-commerce operations, digital marketing, logistics support and online business in Jammu & Kashmir. Strong growth potential.

  • SpecializationsSEO, SEM, Content Marketing, Social Media
  • DurationVaries, Certificates to UG Degrees
  • Entrance ExamsUsually none
