Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS)

Entrance Exam: NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test)

Duration: 5.5 years (4.5 years academic education + 1 year internship)

Career Paths after MBBS

  • General Physician Practice after internship
  • Postgraduate Specialization (MD/MS) 3 years
  • Super Specialization (DM/MCh) 3 years
  • Medical Research Various opportunities
  • Government Medical Services Various positions
×

Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery (BAMS)

Entrance Exam: NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test)

Duration: 5.5 years (4.5 years academic education + 1 year internship)

Career Paths after BAMS

  • Ayurvedic Doctor Practice after internship
  • Postgraduate Specialization (MD Ayurveda) 3 years
  • Panchakarma Specialist Specialized training
  • Ayurvedic Research Various opportunities
  • Government Ayurvedic Services Various positions
×

Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS)

Entrance Exam: NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test)

Duration: 5 years (4 years academic education + 1 year internship)

Career Paths after BDS

  • Dental Surgeon Practice after internship
  • Postgraduate Specialization (MDS) 3 years
  • Orthodontist Specialized training
  • Oral Surgeon Specialized training
  • Government Dental Services Various positions
